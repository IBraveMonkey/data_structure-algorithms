# ‚è∞ Cron Jobs (Scheduler)

Cron allows you to schedule tasks to run automatically at specific times or intervals.

**Syntax**: `min hour day_of_month month day_of_week`

**Example**: `0 3 * * * /path/to/script.sh` (Runs every day at 3:00 AM).

**Control Command**: `crontab -e` (Edits the current user's crontab file).
